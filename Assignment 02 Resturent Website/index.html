Mandi House Resturant

Mandi House is a popular dining venue located in the center of Karachi, known for its authentic Mandi dishes such as Chicken & Mutton Mandi, Madbee, and Madfoon. The restaurant emphasizes a traditional Arabic dining experience, offering a range of flavors and delightful desserts.
